Professional Service and Education

RCBD aims to be a leading big data R&D center, towards the development of national emerging industries such as cloud computing big data and software services.

In addition, RCBD helps to escalate the academic reputation of Beihang University in the field of big data and computer science through exchanges of students and faculties, curricula development and joint international research projects. By collaborating with excellent researchers around the world, the center will provide a magnificent place for talented students to establish their own professional careers path toward big data scientists. Our team is growing into a word-leading research group that is productive in the innovative theoretical and technical area for the R&D of big data.
